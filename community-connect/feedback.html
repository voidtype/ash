Our Complaints Process

We provide services through private enterprise agreements, not as a registered NDIS provider. Our complaints process is designed to be fair, transparent, and focused on resolution.

  1. Submit Your Concern: Use the form above or contact us directly.
  2. Acknowledgement: We'll acknowledge receipt of your complaint within 2 business days.
  3. Investigation: Our team will thoroughly investigate your concerns.
  4. Resolution: We aim to resolve complaints within 10 business days.
  5. Follow-up: We'll check in to ensure you're satisfied with the outcome.

If you're not satisfied with our response, you may escalate your concern to the NDIS Commission or the Commonwealth Ombudsman.
