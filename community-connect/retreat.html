Frequently Asked Questions

Is the retreat suitable for wheelchair users?

Yes, our retreat is fully accessible with wide doorways, ramped access, and an accessible bathroom with grab rails and roll-in shower.

Can I use NDIS funding to pay for my stay?

Yes, for eligible participants, stays can be covered under Core - Assistance with Daily Life or Capacity Building - Increased Social and Community Participation. Please contact us for more information about using your NDIS plan.

Are meals included?

We provide a welcome basket with breakfast items for your first morning. The cabin has a fully equipped kitchen where you can prepare your own meals. Several local cafes and restaurants also offer delivery to the retreat.

What should I bring?

We provide all linens, towels, and basic toiletries. You may want to bring comfortable clothing for outdoor activities, sturdy walking shoes, and any personal items you'll need during your stay.
